About Us

Founded in 2020, ConnectedEmployees emerged from a simple observation: workplace connections were becoming increasingly fragmented in the digital age. Our founders, former HR executives and software engineers, combined their expertise to create a platform that would bridge the gap between traditional office culture and modern remote work environments.

Our mission is to transform workplace relationships through technology that fosters genuine human connections. We believe that engaged, connected employees are the foundation of successful organizations, and we're committed to helping companies build stronger, more collaborative teams through innovative solutions.
